containedJune 28, 2026

High Point, North Carolina

Armed man arrested during Sunday services at Wesley Memorial Church

A community report and layered security response preceded an arrest in the church parking lot. Police reported no injuries.

Confirmed facts

  • High Point Police said a 911 caller reported an armed person in a truck in the church parking lot at approximately 10:15 a.m.
  • Police said an off-duty officer working at the church responded and additional officers arrived within minutes.
  • Police reported that officers disarmed and arrested the man without incident.
  • Police reported finding multiple weapons and more than 500 rounds of ammunition in the vehicle.

What is not established

  • The available police release does not establish a motive or state that criminal intent toward the congregation had been proven.

Preparedness lessons

  • Make it normal for congregants and staff to report unusual behavior promptly.
  • Define who calls emergency services and who meets responding officers.
  • Use layered coverage for parking areas, entrances, and active services.
  • Communicate clearly after an incident, including whether authorities report an ongoing threat.

An arrest or criminal charge is not a conviction. Public descriptions should distinguish allegations, police statements, court findings, and established facts.
